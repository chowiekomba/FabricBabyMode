# 👶Better baby mode

Better baby mode is a recreation of Fundy's baby mode

## 💾Installation
Download the mod on [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/better-baby-mode) and put it in your mods folder.<br>
This mod requires the [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api).

## 🏢Features

<details>
<summary>💀Death Prevention</summary>

- God gives you a boat when in water and saves you from falling into the void in the end
- No fall damage (via feather falling, but you can still sprint jump like normal)
- When suffocating, a block will be broken at the player's head
- getting near lava turns it into obsidian, powder snow turns into water, and magma blocks turn into netherrack
- Monsters die when they get within 4 blocks of the player and say "nope"

</details>

<details>
<summary>🧬Quality of Life</summary>

- All tools, weapons, armor, etc. are enchanted<br>
*Hold an elytra or trident in your main hand to enchant it*
- Looking at a log breaks the log
- Animals respawn when you kill them
- Effects are infinite
- Nether portal can be made out of dirt

</details>

<details>
<summary>⛏️Ores</summary>

### Stone
- 30% chance of dropping coal
- 20% chance of dropping raw copper
- 10% chance of dropping raw iron
- 10% chance of dropping raw gold
- 10% chance of dropping redstone
- 9% chance of dropping lapis
- 7% chance of dropping an emerald
- 4% chance of dropping a diamond

### Deepslate
- 90% chance of no extra ore
- 6% chance of raw iron
- 5% chance of an emerald
- 3% chance of a diamond
- 3% chance of ancient debris

### Diamond
- drops 1000 diamonds

### Ancient Debris
- drops 1000 ancient debris

</details>

<details>
<summary>👾Entities</summary>

### Blaze
- always drops 12 blaze rods

### Wither Skeleton
- always drops his head

### Enderman
- always drops two of his... balls

### Villagers
- always minimum price for trades
</details>

<details>
<summary>💬Chat</summary>

### Blaze Rods
- You can say "I'd love to have some blaze rods, please? :)" for 4 blaze rods

### Ender Pearls
- You can say "I wish I had ender pearls, too" for 4 ender pearls

### Wither or Enderdragon
- You can say "no u" to instantly kill them.

</details>
